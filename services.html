Signature Experience

The Legacy
Session

A full day in your world. Your home, your land, your life — all of it. The books on your shelves, the light through your kitchen window, the garden where you come back to yourself.

Legacy portraits alongside personal branding images built for a woman who has earned her authority — and is finally ready to look like it. We'll catch you in the quiet moments too. The ones between poses where you stop performing and simply are.

The people who belong in this story are welcome. Your partner, your children, your closest friends. Extended family portrait sessions are a natural fit here — generations together, documented in a single unhurried day.

This is a celebration of a whole life.

What's Included
Style and concept session

Before we photograph anything, we sit down together — in person or virtually — to map out your vision, the spaces you want to capture, your wardrobe, and how you want to feel in these images. Nothing is left to chance.

Full day at your home or property

Six to eight unhurried hours in your world. Your kitchen, your study, your garden, your land — we move through all of it together. There is no rushing.

Family and friends are welcome

Your partner, your children, your closest people — they belong in this story too. The Legacy Session is designed to document an entire season of life, and the people who are part of that life are always welcome to be part of the day.

Professional hair and makeup

A talented hair and makeup artist comes to you before we begin. You arrive to your own session already feeling entirely like yourself.

Wardrobe consultation and planning

We work through what you'll wear together — not to create a "look," but to make sure every wardrobe choice feels natural to who you are and what you're building.

Image selection and purchase

Images are selected and purchased individually — so you choose exactly what matters to you. No package pressure, no images you don't want. Just the ones that are truly yours.

Image selection session

We sit together (virtually or in person) to review your images and select what carries your story forward. This is where the magic becomes concrete.

Intimate Session

The Portrait
Session

A more intimate experience with a Wisconsin women's portrait photographer who knows how to slow down. Whether you're in Madison, the Lake Wisconsin area, or anywhere across Central Wisconsin, we'll find your light — chasing golden hour at the end of the day, building a scene around the way you naturally inhabit a space, or simply letting the moment lead. Less about volume, more about depth. Friends and family are welcome here too.

This session is also a beautiful fit for senior portraits — a milestone moment photographed with the same care and intention we bring to every session. No rushed poses, no generic backdrops. Just your senior, in her element, stepping into what's next.

What's Included
Style and concept session

We plan together before the camera ever comes out. Your brand, your audience, how you need to show up — we build the session around that.

Half-day at your location

Three to four focused hours in your home, office, or a meaningful location. We don't rush, and we don't waste time on setups that don't serve you.

Professional hair and makeup

You arrive to your own session looking and feeling exactly like yourself — only more so.

Image selection and purchase

Images are selected and purchased individually. You choose exactly the ones that carry your message — website headers, speaker materials, social, editorial. No pressure to take what you don't need.

Usage rights and high-res files

All images come with full commercial usage rights. Use them anywhere, however you need, for as long as you need them.

The Process

What to expect from start to finish

01 Discovery Call

We talk for 30 minutes — not a sales call. I want to understand your world, what you're stepping into, and whether this is the right fit for both of us.

02 Style Consultation

Before we photograph anything, we map out your vision. The spaces, the wardrobe, how you want to feel in these images. Nothing is left to chance.

03 The Session

I come to you. Unhurried hours in your world — your kitchen, your garden, your study. Hair and makeup arrive first. Then we begin.

04 The Reveal

You see your images for the first time together. We take our time choosing the ones that carry your story. Each image you select is yours — to print, to build with, to carry forward.
