The Sessions

Two ways to be documented.

Signature Experience

The Legacy
Session

A full day. Your home, your land, your life — all of it. The books on your shelves, the light through your kitchen window, the garden where you come back to yourself. Legacy portraits alongside brand images built for a thought leader.

  • Before we photograph anything, we sit down together — in person or virtually — to map out your vision, the spaces you want to capture, your wardrobe, and how you want to feel in these images. Nothing is left to chance.

  • Six to eight unhurried hours in your world. Your kitchen, your study, your garden, your land — we move through all of it together. There is no rushing.

  • A talented hair and makeup artist comes to you before we begin. You arrive to your own session already feeling entirely like yourself.

  • Fine art prints designed to live on your walls for decades. These are the images your grandchildren will know you by.

  • Clean, powerful images for your website, book covers, press features, and speaking profiles. The kind that make people stop scrolling.

  • We sit together and look at your images. You choose what you love. Nothing is decided until that moment.

Focused Experience

The Portrait
Session

Half a day. Intimate and unhurried. We focus on one chapter, one room, one facet of who you are. For the woman who knows exactly what she needs — and is ready for it.

  • Before we photograph anything, we sit down together to talk through your vision, the space, your wardrobe, and how you want to feel. This is where the session takes shape.

  • Three to four focused hours in a space that means something to you. Intimate, unhurried, and entirely yours.

  • A talented hair and makeup artist comes to you before we begin. You arrive to your own session already feeling entirely like yourself.

  • A carefully curated set of images from your session — portraits that feel honest and true to who you are right now, in this chapter.

  • We work together to choose the right image, size, and format. Something made to live in your home — not sit in a folder on your desktop.

  • We sit together and look at your images. You choose what you love. Nothing is decided until that moment.

About Steph Shanks

Some women walk into a room and change the energy. They've built careers, raised families, created something from nothing — and somehow, through all of it, they've never once been photographed the way they deserve.

I'm Stephanie Shanks, a Wisconsin portrait and personal branding photographer with over 20 years of experience. I work with women who are ready — ready to have images that finally match their authority, their warmth, their whole life.

I come to you. Your home, your space, the corners of Wisconsin that mean something to you. And I create portraits that tell the truth about who you are.
